I am a postdoctoral researcher in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at the SENS and SELMET research units at INRAE and CIRAD in Montpellier (France) since September 2022.

My current research is on the engineering of graphical user interfaces and visualizations in socio-ecological contexts, and the practice of research in interaction with societal concerns for various communities. Its main outcomes are:

  • An experimental tool to create interfaces based on the Entity-Component-System model [EICS'19], and a report for engineering communities on the needs of researchers for programming new and original interfaces [EICS'22]
  • Two reports on the use of HCI methods and visualizations in an interdisciplinary territorial governance project [IHM'23, CHI'24]
  • An open-source H.264/AVC video decoder improving speed and code size over the state of the art, to support the evolution of programming practices in the Open Media engineering community and help practically reduce the energy footprint of video streaming

My background is in software engineering, with long-time involvement in HCI and Competitive Programming (CP):

  • Double engineering degree in Computer Science, HCI and CP, from Centrale Marseille and KTH in Sweden (Sept. 2008 – Oct. 2012)
  • R&D engineer in Paris on developping the software for a digital TV recorder (Dec. 2012 – June 2014)
  • Internship supervised by Wendy Mackay on gestural command interfaces in the ex)situ team at Inria Saclay (Mar. 2015 – Aug. 2015)
  • PhD supervised by Stéphane Huot on the programming tools to design interfaces in the Loki team at Inria Lille (Nov. 2015 – Dec. 2019)
  • Research assistant in Algorithmics, Software Engineering and Web Development at Centrale Lyon (Sep. 2019 – Aug. 2022)

Being very concerned about ecology, I have been a willing member of the Sustainable Development and Societal Responsibility local committee at Centrale Lyon, and am now co-chairing the French working group on HCI and ecology. I work hard to explore unconventional paths, most of my spare time going into compulsively crafting code then sharing it with engineering and academic communities.

On a personal note I am a passionate long-distance hiker, bike commuter, hip-hop dancer and choir singer ♫.
