I am a postdoctoral researcher at INRAE and CIRAD in Montpellier, member of research units SENS and SELMET since September 2022. In the past I :

  • obtained a double degree in Engineering and Computer Science from Centrale Marseille and KTH Royal Institute of Technology (September 2008 – October 2012).
  • worked as R&D engineer in Paris on optimizing software for embedded TV decoders (December 2012 – June 2014).
  • did an internship supervised by Wendy Mackay at Inria Saclay (March 2015 – August 2015).
  • did a PhD in Human-Computer Interaction supervised by Stéphane Huot at Inria Lille (November 2015 – December 2019).
  • worked as research assistant at École Centrale de Lyon (September 2019 – August 2022).

My research field is Human-Computer Interaction, where I work on the engineering of graphical user interfaces and visualizations in socio-ecological contexts. My core motivation is to explore and improve the impacts of research on society. I have experimented with writing awarded articles for engineering and research communities (EICS'19, EICS'22), developing and actively promoting an open source project (edge264), co-chairing two working groups on Ecology and Sustainability, and being involved in interdisciplinary action research projects. As a teacher I have given courses on User Interfaces and Competitive Programming, and have been assistant in Algorithmics, Software Engineering and Web Development. I work hard to explore unconventional paths, most of my spare time going into compulsively crafting code then sharing it with engineering and academic communities.

On a personal note I am a passionate hiker, cook, cyclist, and musician ♫.
